About Our Company

HHH Safety - Shahid Hameed Enterprises

Leading manufacturer of premium safety gloves and aprons based in Sialkot - the city of sports and surgical instruments.

15+ Years of Excellence

Owned by Shahid Hameed

Chini Chowk, Sialkot - Pakistan's Premier Industrial City

HHH Safety, under the leadership of Shahid Hameed, has been at the forefront of safety equipment manufacturing since 2009. Located in the heart of Sialkot's industrial district at Chini Chowk, our facility spans over 15,000 square feet of modern manufacturing space.

With a dedicated workforce of 100+ skilled artisans and workers, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to produce world-class safety gloves and aprons that meet international standards.
